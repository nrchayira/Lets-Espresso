Our Story

In the 10 years that have passed we have found our place amongst a growing city as more and more people have found us. We wanted to give the coffee drinking population of Leeds a venue that paid absolute attention to the quality of just two ingredients, coffee and (possibly) milk, and an informative and welcoming environment to enjoy it in. We’ve expanded over the years to include a kitchen that produces some of the best breakfast and lunch options you’ll find in the city centre.

About Us

An espresso bar in its foundation and purpose, we're situated close to Leeds Train Station with many of its commuters relying on us for consistently great coffee drinks. We've grown to become known as one of the best breakfast and brunch offerings in the city as well as serving delicious pastries and sweet options.
